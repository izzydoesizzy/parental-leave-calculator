# Parental Leave Calculator — Canada

> Calculate your parental leave entitlements, benefits, employer top-ups, and plan your family's finances with confidence.

## What Is This?

A comprehensive, free, open-source parental leave calculator for Canadian parents. Models both federal EI and Quebec's QPIP, handles employer top-ups, compares standard vs. extended leave, and provides personalized recommendations with cited sources.

### Features

- **Full EI calculation engine** — maternity (15 weeks), standard parental (40 weeks), and extended parental (69 weeks) with accurate benefit rates and caps
- **Quebec QPIP support** — basic and special plans with higher benefit rates and automatic self-employed coverage
- **Employer top-up modeling** — presets (75%, 80%, 93%, 95%, 100%) and custom configurations for both parents
- **Province selector** — jurisdiction-specific job protection periods for all 10 provinces + federal employees
- **Both parents modeled** — separate salary, top-up, and leave week inputs for birthing parent and other parent
- **Sharing bonus calculation** — automatically calculates the 5-week (standard) or 8-week (extended) use-it-or-lose-it bonus
- **Smart recommendations** — personalized tips based on salary differential, top-up coverage, self-employment status, tenure, and CCB impact
- **Visual timeline** — week-by-week income chart showing benefits and top-ups for both parents
- **7 in-depth articles** — standard vs. extended analysis, dad's leave guide, self-employment guide, top-up negotiation, QPIP deep dive, financial planning, and returning to work
- **Cited sources** — Canada.ca, Statistics Canada, QPIP, CFIB, YWCA, CBC, and employment law references
